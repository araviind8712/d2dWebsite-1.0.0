RNA-Seq Analysis and Gene Regulation

Objective

To analyze RNA sequencing (RNA-Seq) data for uncovering gene regulation patterns and provide comprehensive bioinformatics training to the client, including tools and scripting techniques.

KEY ACTIVITIES

To analyze RNA sequencing (RNA-Seq) data for uncovering gene regulation patterns and provide comprehensive bioinformatics training to the client, including tools and scripting techniques.

  1. RNA-Seq Analysis and Gene Regulation Study
    • Conducted a detailed RNA-Seq analysis to investigate gene expression levels and regulatory mechanisms.
    • Processed raw sequencing data through alignment, quantification, and differential expression analysis.
    • Used advanced analytical approaches to explore gene regulation pathways, identifying key regulatory elements that influence gene expression.
  2. Automated Snakemake Pipeline Development
    • Designed and implemented an automated Snakemake pipeline to streamline RNA-Seq data analysis.
    • Ensured reproducible and efficient data processing, from raw data to final results, reducing manual intervention and minimizing errors.
  3. Tutorial and Training Services

    We provided comprehensive training to equip the client with essential bioinformatics skills:

    • Scripting
      • Delivered hands-on training in scripting techniques to automate data analysis tasks and customize workflows.
    • Documentation
      • Guided the client in creating detailed, reproducible, and shareable workflows using Jupyter Notebooks.
    • SLURM and Bash Scripting
      • Taught the client how to write and optimize SLURM scripts for job scheduling and Bash scripts for command-line tasks.
      • Enhanced the client’s ability to manage computational resources efficiently in high-performance computing (HPC) environments.

OUTCOMES

This project not only provided high-quality analytical results but also empowered the client with valuable skills and knowledge to independently handle future bioinformatics challenges.

Through this project, DNA2Discovery highlighted its commitment to delivering:

  • Technical excellence in bioinformatics solutions.
  • Educational support to ensure long-term client success in research endeavors.

We take pride in blending expertise with education, helping our clients achieve their scientific goals while gaining confidence in bioinformatics practices.
